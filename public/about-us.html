About VectraCore

VectraCore is an analytical workspace built for active currency, stock, and index traders — combining chart reading, Smart Money Concepts, liquidity structure, and real-time market news into one place to think through a setup.

What we're building

Instead of switching between a charting platform, a news feed, and a notes app, VectraCore brings structural chart analysis, macro context, and live market headlines into a single conversational workspace — so you can ask a direct question about a chart or the market and get a grounded answer in one place.

Structure-first analysis

Reads what's actually on your chart — liquidity, structure, and SMC concepts — rather than generic indicator commentary.

Real market context

Pulls live headlines into the conversation so macro questions are grounded in what's actually happening, not guesswork.

Where we are

VectraCore is currently in free public beta. We're actively refining the product based on how traders actually use it — feedback from this stage directly shapes what we build next.
